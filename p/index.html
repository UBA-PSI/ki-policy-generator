AI-Defend v1

AI-Learn v1
AI may be used freely for learning. The exam is supervised without AI.
  • AI tools may be freely used for learning (e.g., exam preparation, explanations).
  • AI may not be used for graded assignments produced without supervision (e.g., homework, term papers, project deliverables).
  • Competence is verified through a supervised examination (e.g., written exam, oral exam) without AI.
  • No labeling of AI use required.

AI-DocLog v1
AI permitted for submissions. Prompt log and reflection required.
  • AI tools may be used for unsupervised graded work – reflective documentation is mandatory.
  • Prompt log and personal reflection are part of every submission.
  • The quality of AI use (prompts, review, further processing) is included in the assessment.
  • Competence is verified through a supervised examination without AI.

AI-Defend v1
AI without restrictions. Competence verified through supervised exam.
  • AI tools may be used without restrictions – including for all unsupervised graded work.
  • No labeling of AI use required.
  • Competence is verified through a supervised examination (oral or written), in which the submitted work must be represented.
  • Those who cannot convincingly represent their work must expect a significantly lower grade.
AI-Defend v1 →
AI-Skill v1
AI competence is a learning goal. Process and result are assessed.
  • AI competence is an explicit learning objective – AI tools should be used actively and reflectively.
  • Prompt log with reflection is a mandatory component and is graded.
  • The quality of AI use is included in the assessment.
  • A portfolio documents AI use throughout the semester.

AI free for learning. Examination without AI.

  • KI darf nicht bei bewerteten Arbeiten eingesetzt werden, die ohne Aufsicht erstellt werden (z.B. Hausaufgaben, Seminararbeiten, Projektabgaben).
  • Der Kompetenznachweis erfolgt durch eine Prüfung unter Aufsicht (z.B. Klausur, mündliche Prüfung) ohne KI.
  • Keine Kennzeichnung der KI-Nutzung erforderlich.
  • AI tools may be freely used for learning (e.g., exam preparation, explanations).
  • AI may not be used for graded assignments produced without supervision (e.g., homework, term papers, project deliverables).
  • Competence is verified through a supervised examination (e.g., written exam, oral exam) without AI.
  • No labeling of AI use required.
